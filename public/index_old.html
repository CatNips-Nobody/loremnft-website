How will people gain access to this Discord?
Our Discord will remain closed, but will allow new members two different ways, via Resident Nomination as well as an allocation of spots for our exciting new Partnerships.
How does the Resident Nomination system work?
Residents will each gain the opportunity to invite 5 of their trusted friends and associates to join the LOREM world as Initiates. As Initiates, they can explore this world, participate in the shenanigans and enjoy all LOREM has to offer, however they do not automatically have a spot on the AL.
How can I earn my spot on the Allowlist?
Closer to mint day, Residents will nominate Initiates to gain access to a Rystal for AL. Initiates will be nominated based on their quality of engagement, investment in the lore and a genuine connection with the rest of the community. There are no level systems here, so spam messaging will likely not yield the best result.
